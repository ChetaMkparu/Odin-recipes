Description

Ingriedients

  • Eggs
  • Fresh tomatoes and pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and seasoning cubes
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs and beat with a fork with salt and pepper
  2. Chop your fresh tomatoes and pepper and fry with vegetable oil in a pan for 5-7 minutes
  3. Add your egg mixture and fry till light brown
  4. Serve with boiled yam or bread
