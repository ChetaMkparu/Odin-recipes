Description

Ingriedients

  • Rice
  • Fresh tomatoes and pepper
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Chicken or Turkey
  • Thyme and curry
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt and seasoning cubes
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Blend your fresh tomatoes and pepper into a paste
  2. Cook your chicken and turkey and preserve the stock
  3. Fry your tin tomato paste with onions and ginger for 10 minutes
  4. Add bayleaf for extra scent and flavour
  5. Add your blended fresh tomato mix and combine together
  6. Add your seasoning and rice, and stir them together
  7. Pour your chicken stock and small water then leave to cook
